About Us

AJW Air was founded in 2024 by Aniruddh Singh and Agastya Menon as part of a Design Thinking project given by their teacher, Mrs. Kulkarni. Inspired by the challenge to create something innovative, they envisioned an airline that would combine luxury with affordability, making high-end travel accessible to more people.

Aniruddh and Agastya, both passionate about aviation and customer experience, worked tirelessly to turn their school project into a reality. AJW Air is the result of their commitment to rethinking air travel, with a focus on providing passengers with cost-effective luxury.

Their journey from the classroom to the skies is a testament to their dedication and innovative spirit, and AJW Air continues to reflect the values they learned through the guidance of Mrs. Kulkarni.
